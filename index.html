Habitat

Tigers live in forests and swamps. They do not live far away from water.

Range

Tigers live in india and nepal.

Food

Tigers eat meat. They kill: deer, wild pigs, young buffalo and young elephants. When they are really hungry they eat: fowl, fish, lizards ,frogs, crocodile, carrion and even humans

Sumatran tiger
